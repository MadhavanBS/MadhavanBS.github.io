Developed a Python-based RAG chatbot for intelligent document analysis using LangChain and Ollama's Llama 3.2 model. It enables contextual question answering across multiple documents by implementing a hybrid retrieval pipeline that combines BGE embeddings, BM25, and FAISS.

Tiny Recursive Models: Paper Implementation

View Repository

We reimplemented the Tiny Recursive Model (TRM) from scratch and studied how different design choices affect its ability to solve Sudoku puzzles through recursive reasoning. We ran more than 60 experiments to analyze the impact of data augmentation, recursion depth, model size, attention, and other hyperparameters on accuracy and generalization. Our results showed that deeper recursive reasoning improved performance much more than simply making the model larger, highlighting the power of small recursive models for reasoning tasks.
